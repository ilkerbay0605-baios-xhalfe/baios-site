Integration Philosophy

🇹🇷 Türkçe

BAIOS does not integrate everything. That is not a shortcoming — it is a deliberate choice.

The principles behind how we handle the connections that touch your business.

1. Just Tell It

You don't decide how each connection gets built. You say what you want, and BAIOS picks the right path.

You say "I want to see my invoices." BAIOS checks whether a trusted path we have prepared exists; if it does, it sets that up. If it doesn't, it prepares a flow made just for you — and it tells you plainly which path it chose and why.

You never make a technical decision. One sentence is enough.

2. Two Kinds of Connections

In BAIOS, connections come in two forms:

Ready modules. The connections that touch your money, your customers, your legal records — accounting, invoicing, collections, official correspondence. We are preparing these, and will test and maintain them; we design them around KVKK. You only connect.

These modules are **in the preparation stage.** The first connection (Logo İşbaşı) is being tested; the rest will roll out in turn.

Made-for-you flows. When you have a niche need that isn't on the list, BAIOS prepares it for you — on your own machine, under your own control. You don't have to wait.

You never choose between the two; BAIOS picks the right one based on the nature of the work.

3. Why We Don't Integrate Everything

We never present anything as "ready" that we cannot stand behind.

Our ready modules are few and carefully made. We treat every connection that touches your business with care. Trusted connections instead of thousands of shallow ones.

This is not "more is better"; it is "more trustworthy is better."

4. No Free Rein on Risky Work

We do not let the AI build the connections that touch your money, your customers, and your legal records on its own.

Connections like these — issuing an invoice, taking a payment, sending official correspondence — run through modules that we prepare in advance and design to be auditable. Where the outcome cannot be undone and carries legal weight, there is no room for guesswork.

This is not a limitation — it is care. If the work is serious, it is taken seriously.

5. Why This Choice?

For every connection, BAIOS shows you which path it took and why.

When it prepares something for you, it shows what it is about to do step by step, in plain language, before it runs; you see it, you approve it, then it runs. No black box — every step is visible, and you can object to any of them.

6. Own It

Your connections run on your machine, with your keys, under your control.

  • Your data stays with you.
  • A connection is set up once, then runs by the rules.
  • Even if you leave BAIOS one day, the setup you built is yours.
**Author once, run forever — own it, don't rent it.**

A Layer That Works Beside You

This approach follows naturally from our philosophy. BAIOS does not act in your place; it works beside you, transparently and in a way you can undo. Integrations are the same: not fast but trustworthy, not many but carefully made, not ours but under your control.
