TL;DR: Sign up to the announcement list for the BAIOS Edge beta program.

Edge Beta

🇹🇷 Türkçe

BAIOS Edge is not ready for general release yet, but the beta program is opening. If you sign up:

  • **First beta notification** — be the first to know when it's ready.
  • **Hardware compatibility list** — once it's clear which devices are supported.
  • **Prototype updates** — monthly progress summary.
  • **Beta participant call** — invitation for pilot use.

Sign-Up

Email: edge@baios.dev

Put "Edge Beta Sign-Up" in the subject. It speeds things up if you share the following in your message:

  • Which hardware you have (Pi 4/5, Jetson, ESP32, robot, etc.)
  • The scenario you have in mind (smart home, sensor, robot, maker)
  • Contact preference (is email enough, would you join a Telegram/Discord group)

When?

Edge development is active. There's no fixed date, but the targets are:
